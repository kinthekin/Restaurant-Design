    Official Pizza Goat About

    Official Pizza Goat Global is a recently opened chain, but one sure to explode.

    Here, we take pizza seriously. Our objective is to provide genuine, high quality pizza with no bullshit. Founded in 2013, we have quickly amassed a loyal customer base.

    Our selection is inspired by pizzas from around the Globe, valuing the culture behind the food more than anything else.

    From the very beginning, we’ve embraced innovation without losing sight of tradition.
    Our pizzas are crafted with the finest ingredients, carefully selected to deliver bold, memorable flavors in every bite. We experiment with unique toppings, artisanal techniques, and inventive recipes — all while keeping the heart of pizza alive: that perfect combination of dough, sauce, cheese, and creativity.

    Official Pizza Goat isn’t just a pizza joint—it’s a place where friends become regulars, families share laughs, and every slice tells a story.
    Whether you're here for a classic pepperoni, a creative special, or just a good time with great company, we’re proud to serve up the flavor, warmth, and community spirit that makes Offical Pizza Goat what it is today.

    We take pride in being more than just a restaurant; we’re a part of the neighborhood.
    From sponsoring local teams to hosting community nights and fundraisers, Official Pizza Goat has always been about giving back to the folks who helped make us who we are.

    So whether you’re stopping in for a quick lunch, grabbing takeout on the way home, or sitting down to celebrate something special, know that every bite you take is made with care, tradition, and a little bit of the Goats magic. Because around here, pizza isn’t just food—it’s family.

    Over the years, we’ve grown from a local favorite to a global presence, but our core values remain unchanged: quality, creativity, and a commitment to delivering the best pizza experience possible.
    Whether you’re sharing a pie with friends, grabbing a quick slice, or discovering new flavors, we’re here to make every moment deliciously unforgettable.
